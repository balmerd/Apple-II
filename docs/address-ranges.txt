ADDRESS RANGES

HARDWARE           $BFFF TO $FFFF
DOS                $9600 TO HARDWARE
USER2              $6000 TO DOS
PAGE2              $4000 TO USER2
PAGE1              $2000 TO PAGE2
USER1              $0800 TO PAGE1
TEXT SCREEN AND OS $0000 TO USER1

HI-RES SCREEN COORDINATES
WIDTH: 40 BYTES WITH 7 PIXELS PER BYTE
HEIGHT: 192 LINES
RESOLUTION: 7 * 40 (280) X 192

ZERO-PAGE POINTERS
